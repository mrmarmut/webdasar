Hakari Atsuko

Atsuko Hakari (秤アツコ in Japanese) is one of the characters in the 2021 roleplay game Blue Archive who studies in Arius Branch School (or Arius Satellite School) and wields a Submachine Gun. She originally acted as the supporting antagonist for the third chapter of the third volume of the main story, the "Eden Treaty", she later played as the rescued in the fourth chapter and as the supporting protagonist in the final volume, "Where All Miracles Begin". She is also one of the members of the Arius Squad, an elite group of Arius students.

Profile

Introduction

One of the four members of Arius Branch School's elite Arius Squad. She's silent and always communicates through sign language, but perhaps there's more to this than meets the eye. Her squadmates call her "princess," and Saori in particular seems very protective of her.

Personality

Atsuko may be very silent, but she has a kind heart. Compared to the rest of the Arius Squad and possibly all of Arius, Atsuko appears to be the kindest, having a personality that can be considered quite exceptional in Arius.

Firearm

She utilize a CZ Scorpion Evo 3 A1 with an Arius logo decal on the right side of the receiver, and a decal of the school's motto on the left side.

Meme

After her 4-koma debut, a grin was added by fans via editing, which led to her becoming one of the most popular memes in the game and its artworks. This meme is later dubbed "Atsuko's Grin", and has become prevalent in many fan works.
